The @StiffnessMatrixType let the user specifiy choose type of
stiffness matrix that shall be given by the mechanical behaviour and
that will be used by the resolution algorithm.

This keyword is followed by a string. The following values are
allowed:
- Elastic (undammaged)
- SecantOperator (damaged)
- TangentOperator
- ConsistantTangentOperator

@Example:
@StiffnessMatrixType 'Elastic';
